About

21 December, 2022

My name is Kush Patel, and I am the Analytics Platform (AP) Manager at Snap Finance. We aim to enable, implement, and accelerate advanced analytics across the business by collaborating with Product Managers and engineers. We focus on understanding their needs and providing them with the tools and platforms that simplify their workflows and enhance efficiency. I lead a talented team comprising Machine Learning (ML) engineers, Data Scientists, Business Intelligence specialists, and Software Engineers.

One of our key innovations is the Machine Learning and Experimentation Platform (MAEPL). MAEPL empowers users to run better experiments and seamlessly transform them into models. It establishes standardized data contracts across ML, experimentation, distribution, permutation, combination, etc. This abstraction allows consumers to focus on their work without worrying about its complexities. MAEPL also makes it easy for us to upgrade to the model easily.

We have also developed the Analytics Platform Ecosystem (APE), a suite of tools and libraries designed to streamline our ML workflows and support model building and deployment. APE simplifies workflows for Data Scientists while enabling analytics to scale effectively and meet business needs.

MAEPL and APE have enabled possibilities and growth previously impossible in business.
